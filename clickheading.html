Bir Amarsingh School

Bir amarsingh school is located in Charpane,Beldangi Jhapa Nepal. It has recently started the teaching of technical education in computer Engineering subjects to studets of class 9 and 10. Those who are interested can join the school every annual year in first month. You will need to pass an entrance exam which constitutes 4 major subjects like English, Nepali, Maths and Science. The wish of Biramarsingh family is quality education. Interested students can apply to study school in any class on general faculty.
